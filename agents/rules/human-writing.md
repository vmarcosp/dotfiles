When writing or rewriting documents, comments, READMEs, or any prose — use the `human-writing` skill to produce clean, natural text free of AI-generated patterns.

## Steps

1. Invoke the `human-writing` skill for any document or prose writing task
2. Avoid em dashes, excessive bold, over-structured tables, and deep nesting
3. Write direct, conversational sentences — no filler, no hedging
4. Match the user's voice: direct, bilingual-aware (en-US / pt-BR), technically precise
5. Never produce multi-paragraph docstrings or bloated comment blocks
